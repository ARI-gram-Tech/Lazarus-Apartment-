Welcome to Lazarus Apartments

Located at Grands Mill Road near Ari Gram Center, Lazarus Apartments offers premium living spaces with world-class amenities. Our 13-floor building features modern apartments, a hotel on the 2nd floor, a rooftop swimming pool, and a relaxing bistro.

Tenants enjoy free car parking while visitors pay only Ksh 300. Our hall space on the first floor is available for rental for events lasting not more than 5 days.
